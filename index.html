RAMONES

  • 1965 - John Cummings and Thomas Erdelyi play in a high-school garage band, known as the Tangerine Puppets.

  • 1974 - The Ramones are founded John and Thomas, who invite the friend Jeffrey Hyman to join the band. In the same year, they play 74 times for the CBGB club by the end of the year.

  • 1976 - They record their self-entitled album, which contains the famous Blitzkrieg Bop.

  • 1977 - Leave home and Rocket to Russia are released.

  • 1978 - Tommy leaves the band and is replaced by Marky. In the same year Road to Ruinis released with the famous song I Wanna Be Sedated.

  • 1983 - Marky Ramone is fired due to the alcoholism and replaced by Richie Ramone.

  • 1987 - Marky, now clean and sober, return to the band.

  • 1988 - Record the album Brain Drain which contain their highest-charting hit in America Pet Semetary.

  • 1996 - Play their last concert in Hollywood.
